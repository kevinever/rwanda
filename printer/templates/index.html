How does it work with PREVIEW ?

Create an account

To get started you will need to own or create an acount.This account will help you to manage your printers toner request and delivery order,it will enable you to request and create the printer's toner delivery order,to order them ,to cancel them when you no longer need to recieve an order,to change the destination of the parcel as well as tracking the your parcel. It will also help you get notification of where your parcel is in case the location changes.

Access the dashboard

The dashboard is the your working place,you will be able to create your printer's delivery order for anytime. Complete the form and hit order, the order delivey should be displayed in the recent delivery order. Hit order to make an order for your first parcel order,Hit change destination to change the destination of your parcel delivery order, Hit cancel to cancel your parcel delivery order in case you no longer need it to be delivered.

Order and Receive

Once you hit order you will need to wait between few hours and your delivery will be there
